Sweet November Bistro Menu

Appetizers

  • Puff Puff

    Soft, deep-fried dough balls, golden and crispy on the outside, light and fluffy on the inside.

  • Suya

    Skewered spiced beef (or chicken) grilled to perfection, served with sliced onions, tomatoes, and spicy pepper sauce.

  • Pepper Soup

    A spicy and aromatic broth made with fish (or goat meat), seasoned with spices and herbs.

Main Courses

  • Jollof Rice

    A rich and flavorful rice dish cooked in a blend of tomatoes, onions, and a medley of spices, served with fried plantains and grilled chicken or beef.

  • Efo Riro

    A delicious spinach stew cooked with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and your choice of meat (beef, chicken, or fish), served with pounded yam or rice.

  • Egusi Soup

    A hearty and flavorful soup made with ground melon seeds, cooked with spinach, onions, and your choice of meat or fish, Served with fufu or semovita.

Desserts

  • Chin Chin

    Crunchy, lightly sweetened fried dough pieces.

  • Coconut Rice Cake

    A delicious cake made with grated coconut, sugar, and flour, with a moist and slightly chewy texture.

  • Akara

    Fried bean cakes made from ground black-eyed peas, onions, and spices, served warm and crispy, served with pap.
